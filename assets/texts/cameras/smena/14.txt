In 1961 GOMZ, Leningrad abandons production of the original bakelite SMENA in favor of bodies molded in thermoplastic (SMENA-5).

MMZ then takes over production of the former GOMZ SMENA-4 but with lower quality, without a lever wind, and with new, normalized shutter speeds: B; 1/8 - 1 /250s and a self timer.
